# Zeutara Execution-Strain Engine

## Overview

This prototype analyzes early-stage startups for signs of execution strain and produces a priority score, likely bottleneck, Zeutara advisory angle, and personalized outbound draft.

The purpose is to support Zeutara’s business development pipeline by identifying founders who may be entering moments where execution architecture becomes urgent.

## Why this component matters

Lead scraping and outbound automation are increasingly commoditized. The more valuable question is not simply “Who can we contact?” but “Which founders are experiencing operational strain that makes Zeutara relevant right now?”

This component was selected because it is the intelligence layer that determines whether the pipeline behaves like a strategic advisory acquisition engine instead of a generic outbound machine.

## How it works

The engine evaluates signals such as:

- employee growth
- founder language around scaling
- coordination or prioritization problems
- GTM and RevOps hiring
- recent funding stage

It then returns:

- execution strain score
- priority level
- likely bottleneck
- recommended Zeutara advisory angle
- outbound draft
- reasoning summary
